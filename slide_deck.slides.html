Behaviour of Customer of Ford GoBike System in 2018¶

by Abdulrahman Alkhaldi¶

Investigation Overview¶

In this investigation, I wanted to look at the behaviour of the customers of Ford GoBike System that could be used to minimizing the cost of operating the stations and the othr facilities. The main focus was on the gender and the time related columns (Month, Day and Hour).

Dataset Overview¶

The document consists of information regarding approximately 1.86M bike rides made in a bike-sharing system (Ford GoBike) from fiscal year 2018. Including the trip start/end time, start/end station, duration in seconds as well as additional information such as user type, gender, and birth year.

Bikes Monthly Usage¶

There is season has a siginficant effect. At summer seasion, the usage is at its peak, most likely because of the tourists. However, there is a drop at winter season, prbably due to the bad weather conditions.

Usage Time for Each User Type¶

  • Subscribers: Use bikes mainly on weekdays and at work timings. So, they clearly use it to commute to work.

    • Weekdays: Most rides are at (8 AM - 9 AM) and (5 PM - 6 PM), and its peak is at (Tuesdays, 8 AM).
    • Weekends: There is a significant drop in the demand of bikes compared to weekdays.

  • Customers: Use bikes more often on weekends for touring and leisure.

    • Weekdays: Most rides are at (8 AM - 9 AM) and (5 PM - 6 PM), and its peak is at (Friday, 5 PM).
    • Weekends: Most rides are at (10 AM - 7 PM), and its peak is at (Saturday, 2 PM).
