for month in range(1,13):
    month_string = str(month)
    month_leading_zero = month_string.zfill(2)
    
    bike_data_url = 'https://s3.amazonaws.com/fordgobike-data/2018' + month_leading_zero + '-fordgobike-tripdata.csv.zip'
    response = get(bike_data_url)
        
    # Open zip file, then put extracted zip file into the folder
    unzipped_file = ZipFile(BytesIO(response.content))
    unzipped_file.extractall(folder_name_of_csvs)

What is the structure of your dataset?¶

There were around 1,860,000 bike rides in the dataset that was in 2018 at the greater San Francisco Bay area. The dataset contained features about the trip, bike, station and the member. Most variables in the dataset are numeric.

The dataset was further enhanced with features that I may find neccessary to perform interesting analysis: A few features (start_month, start_day, start_hour, member_age) were added by me due to their usefulness.

What is/are the main feature(s) of interest in your dataset?¶

I am interested in finding the relation between age and using a bike. Also, I am curious to know wether subscribers use the bikes more than regular customers.

What features in the dataset do you think will help support your investigation into your feature(s) of interest?¶

I expect that the starting day will have the strongest effect on the bikes usage. Also, the hour will have a significant effect.
